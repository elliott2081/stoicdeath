About

Why have an app that reminds me of my own mortality?

The purpose of this app is to neither think about death as something depressing, nor to celebrate death or think about it as something “good”. It is an app designed to send a daily reminder that your most important resource in life is your time. By reflecting on the limited nature of our life every morning the ancient Greek and Roman philosophical school of stoicism taught that the truly important things in life would be brought into focus and our attention brought into the present moment.

This app is 70% or so, direct quotes from greek and roman stoic philosophers. The rest are modern people who were influenced by Stoicism, or other philosophers, (such as Socrates) that influenced Stoicism.
